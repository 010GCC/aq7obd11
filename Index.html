Quick Start Guide

The fastest, safest path to meaningful improvements on your facelift 4M Q7.

1. Disable Auto Start/Stop
The single most popular and satisfying first mod on 2021 Q7s. Engine stays running at stops.
→ Search “Start Stop” in OBDeleven One-Click Apps
2. Needle Sweep on Ignition
Instant premium feel. Gauges perform a dramatic sweep every time you start the car — very 2021 RS-like character.
→ One-Click App or Module 17, Byte 1 Bit 0
3. Passenger Mirror Dip
Extremely practical on a large SUV. Mirror automatically tilts down in reverse for perfect curb view and wheel protection.
→ Search “Mirror dip” or “Spiegelabsenkung”
4. G-Meter in Virtual Cockpit
Adds real-time G-force display. Makes your Premium Plus feel more engaging and technical — pairs beautifully with Dynamic mode.
→ One-Click or Module 17 coding
First Steps (Do These)
  • • Battery Maintainer: Use a smart 12V AGM-compatible maintainer (5–10A ideal). Recommended: CTEK MXS 5.0 / 7.0, NOCO Genius5/10, or Battery Tender Plus with AGM mode.
  • • Backup all modules in OBDeleven (critical)
  • • Start with Easy difficulty mods only
  • • Do one mod at a time and test thoroughly
Your 2021 Q7 uses an AGM battery — always select AGM mode on the maintainer.
